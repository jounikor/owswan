2002/05/22

DOS/32 Advanced DOS Extender is released to the public in the form of a "Liberty Edition." The Liberty Edition includes precompiled binaries of DOS/32 Advanced DOS Extender version 7.1 and its tools, the complete set of on-line documentation manuals and is accompanied by the complete and the original source code. DOS/32 Advanced DOS Extender Liberty Edition is released under an Apache-like license which allows unlimited, unrestricted, royalty-free distribution and use of the DOS Extender in any software projects including commercial products, free of any charge.
